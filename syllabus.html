SLAYout: A Graphic Design Theory Bootcamp

This is a 5-session Graphic Design Theory course designed to help designers and beginners alike to have a strong foundation on the fundamentals of visual design.

At the end of the course, participants are expected to:

  • Have a strong understanding of Graphic Design Theory
  • Apply Graphic Design Theory to design work
  • Perceive and critique designs using the correct terminologies to improve their or others' designs
